Products and Info

Products, services, components and information links

Providers of  Real-Time text products and software using Real-Time Text, services, information, etc.

Interoperability with industry standard protocols is strongly encouraged, to ensure unrestricted use of Real-Time Text regardless of networks and systems. Standard protocols include SIP and RFC 4103, as well as XEP-0301 for Jabber/XMPP.
