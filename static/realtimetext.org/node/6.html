Real-Time Text in the Internet and Telecommunication Standards

Summary of Standards and specifications.

This is an overview of documents, overviews, presentations and specifications related to accessible real-time conversation, including Real-Time Text, Total Conversation and Text Telephony. This webpage has the ambition to serve as a convenient starting point for finding information relevant to Real-Time Text.

Due the nature of the work in the standards groups, where standards move on, and new versions of the referenced specifications may take effect. It is advisable for implementers to retrieve the latest versions directly from the standard bodies and verify that the version is still valid.

  • For 3GPP, the specifications can be found under http://www.3gpp.org/specifications
  • For ITU-T, the recommendations can be found under http://www.itu.int/ITU-T/publications/recs.html
  • For IETF, the specifications can be found under https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/
  • For ETSI, the specifications can be found under http://www.etsi.org/WebSite/Standards/Standard.aspx
  • For XMPP, the XMPP extensions can be found under http://www.xmpp.org/extensions/.

    Most 3GPP specifications related to Real-Time Text, Total Conversation and Text Telephony are accepted as ETSI technical specifications and are listed under 3GPP and ETSI below.
