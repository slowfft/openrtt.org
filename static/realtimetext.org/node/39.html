TIA: Accessibility of Real Time Text by the Disabled Community

Submitted by arnoud_R3TF on Mon, 02/07/2011 - 00:00

Real Time Text is now a new focus for the ICT industry and allows many persons who are deaf and hard of hearing to communicate at a level that is equal to what is possible with voice. TIA hosted a round table discussion to bring together members of the manufacturing sector of ICT and representatives of the disabled community. This round table discusion has been captured on video with key persons in the discussion.

The Real-Time Text Taskforce is looking forward to work with members of TIA to ensure that RTT meets the communication needs for all users and can be used on all kinds of networks and telecommunication devices and systems.
